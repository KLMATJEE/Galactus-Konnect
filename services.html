What we offer

Risk management is a very important aspect in our daily repairs/installations, because it allows a company/clients to measure and better understand its current position in terms of risk. It also allows a company/client to define priorities and implement measures to decrease risk to an acceptable level, based on the company's core business activities, its mission plus vision and legal requirements.

Galactus Konnect provides Automation Services and Technical support to preserve the desired level of confidentiality, integrity and availability of a company's IT systems and data. We support the company's critical business processes and data from an IT perspective.
